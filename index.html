Mission Statement

As a group of individuals dedicated to technology, we inspire everyone to explore all potentials and possibilities in tech.We help each other navigate our career paths by sharing experiences and advice on selecting classes, finding opportunities, preparing for interviews, and more. We're a diverse, tight-knit group of people who can rely on each other for not only career and academic advice, but also for social and emotional support.
